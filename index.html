About me

The Thomas Collections' first signature collection was introduced in 2004. We are a US based company that's been working in the background developing revolutionary ideas while patiently waiting for our time to strike.

Thomas Collection products will reflect the quality and exclusiveness associated with the brand. The company will use top grade materials such as cashmere, silks, wool blends, and high gauge denim. Thomas Collection is designed with passion for well made, sophisticated, trend setting garments.

At Thomas Collection Clothing we focus on high quality distribution of our products along with excellent customer service. As innovators we use our abilities to create and acquire new ideas, market strategies and services. Thomas Collection offers cutting edge products as well as a superior selection of merchandise, new innovative products, and focuses on the highest quality of customer service. The Thomas Collection can successfully provide consumers with an enhanced shopping experience.
